A tour around the rescue!

Prepare
Step 1
We prepare for the foxes
We prepare for fox pups by having all supplies such as formula, feeding syringes, nipples, and blankets. When rescuing foxes over 7 weeks old, we need large spaces and vet appointments ready.
Rescue
Step 2
We rescue foxes from anywhere
We rescue foxes from many different situations. We rescue sick, injured, and orphaned foxes from furfarms. Ones that the farmers believe would not make it. We take in surrendered foxes that were once pets and originally came from breeders. We also take in and rescue found pet foxes.
